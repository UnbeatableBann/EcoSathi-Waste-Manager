Our Motivation

Introducing Ecosathi: Revolutionizing Waste Management for All

In response to the widespread issue of improper waste disposal and citizens' difficulties in voicing concerns, we present HexaClean. Our user-friendly app enables people to easily find nearby bins, ensuring cleaner surroundings. Additionally, we've tailored a specialized feature for businesses, such as hotels and restaurants, streamlining their garbage pickup process. HexaClean isn't just a software solution; it's a community-driven initiative promoting cleaner cities, efficient waste management, and direct communication between citizens and the government.
